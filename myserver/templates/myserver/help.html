Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Tap 'Library User' login screen and tap 'Forgot Password?' to reset.

How do I contact support?

Email librarylink.support@hotmail.com. or visit our app Library Link.

How can I borrow a book?

Search for a book, tap 'Add to Book Cart' see if it's available, and then tap 'Borrow' to borrow a book.

Where can I view my borrowed items?

Go to the Borrowing History' section to view all your borrowed books along with reserve books.

How do I search for books?

Go to the 'Library' and Tap the search bar at the top of the home screen to find books by title, author, ISBN, published data, or publisher.

Is there a limit to how many books I can borrow?

Yes, users can borrow up to 5 books at a time.
